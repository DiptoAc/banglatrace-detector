    Method

    A hybrid detector designed for stronger generalization

    Private local mode uses normalized Bengali word and character patterns learned from the repository data. Advanced mode adds multilingual semantic review of discourse structure, formulaic progression, stylistic uniformity, and human idiosyncrasy.

    The two independent signals are combined according to semantic confidence. This reduces reliance on exact phrases seen in the small training set and improves the assessment of polished, previously unseen model output.

    Responsible interpretation

    A detector score is not proof of authorship

    01

    Editing changes the signal

    Human revision, translation, paraphrasing, or mixed human–LLM writing can reduce reliability.

    02

    Short and code-mixed text is harder

    Informal spelling, Romanized Bengali, and Bengali–English mixing require cautious interpretation.

    03

    Use contextual human review

    Do not accuse, penalize, or reject a person based only on this percentage. Review provenance and writing-process evidence.
